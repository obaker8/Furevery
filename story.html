OUR STORY

Read our Furevery story!

 Dogs have been our best friends for centuries. Now, more than ever, the world is realizing how special these four-legged creatures truly are. They are our constant companions, our inexpensive therapy, our instant stress reliever and even the mere act of looking at your pet increases the amount of oxytocin, the “feel good” chemical, in the brain! It is scientifically proven that dogs make us happier. Here at Furevery, we’re passionate about making it possible for everyone to experience the joy of dog parenthood and we’re committed to providing dog parents with the tools needed to raise happy, healthy pups.


Whether you’re looking for a top rated veterinarian, groomer, trainer, dog resort, daycare, or a dog-friendly restaurant, Furevery connects you directly with dog businesses who will treat your dog like family. We are transforming the way you find and communicate with dog businesses and dog lovers, in your community. Furevery has been created by dog parents for dog parents.
